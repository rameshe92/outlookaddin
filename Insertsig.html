Sigsync is a centralized, company-wide Office 365 email signatures service. This client side Outlook Add-in is designed to add Signatures to your emails directly in your Outlook without routing emails through Sigsync Azure Services.
Follow the below steps to enable Sigsync client side mode signatures:

  1. Login to Sigsync dashboard
  2. Add your Office 365 tenant
  3. Create your signature templates
  4. Assign signature template to Rule.
  5. Reload / Refresh Sigsync Signatures add-in
That's it!
